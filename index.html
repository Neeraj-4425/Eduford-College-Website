About the Program

Our Undergraduate programs span 3 to 4 years and are crafted to provide students with a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practice. Whether you're passionate about technology, business, arts, or science — we have a program for you.

All UG programs are affiliated with the University of Eduford and recognized by the UGC (University Grants Commission), ensuring your degree carries national and international value.

Program Highlights

  • Industry-aligned curriculum updated every semester
  • Over 50 specializations to choose from
  • Internship guaranteed in 3rd year at partner companies
  • Exchange programs with 12 international universities
  • Dedicated placement cell with 500+ hiring partners
  • Scholarships available for meritorious students

About the Program

Our Degree programs are designed for students who want a structured, comprehensive academic experience with clear career pathways. These programs are recognized globally, making your qualification valuable whether you stay in India or pursue international opportunities.

Each degree program integrates live projects, industry mentorship, and certification modules from global tech partners including Google, Microsoft, and IBM.

Program Highlights

  • Globally accredited degrees accepted in 40+ countries
  • Live project-based learning from semester 1
  • Certifications from Google, Microsoft, IBM included
  • Dedicated career counseling sessions every month
  • Alumni network of 80,000+ professionals worldwide
  • Hostel and transportation facilities on all campuses

Post Graduate Courses

Take your expertise to the next level with specialized PG programs designed for professionals and aspiring researchers.

Home › Courses › Post Graduate

About the Program

Our Postgraduate programs are tailored for those seeking to deepen their knowledge in a specific discipline or transition into a new field. With evening and weekend batches available, working professionals can upskill without pausing their careers.

Our PG programs are taught by a mix of full-time professors and visiting faculty from top companies and IITs, giving you the best of academia and industry.

Program Highlights

  • Evening & weekend batches for working professionals
  • Research lab access and funding for thesis projects
  • Industry mentors assigned to every student
  • 100% scholarship for UG graduates from Eduford
  • Executive networking events with CXOs every quarter
  • Joint degree options with partner universities abroad
